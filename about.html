Hello! My name's Cian...

I'm a 21 year old programmer studying Computer Science in UCC. My love for technology runs deep, way back to when i was just a young kid pulling apart my electronic toys just to see how they worked. After discovering my parents old consoles I became obsessed with the seemingly endless possibilities of digital worlds and spend the next decade exploring them. I have deep love and admiration of vintage electronics such as CRTs and retro consoles as well as any "legacy" web visuals.
